Resources & Proceedings

Publications, proceedings, and related materials

Conference Proceedings

Since 2015, SampTA conference proceedings have been published and indexed in IEEE Xplore. Earlier contributions were published in special issues of the journal Sampling Theory in Signal and Image Processing (STSIP).

IEEE Xplore Proceedings

  • SampTA 2023 Proceedings — New Haven, U.S.A.
  • SampTA 2019 Proceedings — Bordeaux, France
  • SampTA 2017 Proceedings — Tallinn, Estonia
  • SampTA 2015 Proceedings — Washington, D.C., U.S.

Journal

Sampling Theory in Signal and Image Processing (STSIP) is an international journal closely associated with the SampTA conference series. The journal publishes original research articles and survey papers on all aspects of sampling theory and its applications.
